50-50% of the weight

- Empty stance

10-90% of the weight

- Step out (keep weight on right)

10-90% of the weight

- Bow stance (shift weight towards left)

60-40% of the weight

- Shift weight back

10-90% of the weight

- Shift weight to left

- Step into empty stance

90-10% of the weight

- Continue {3 - 7}
